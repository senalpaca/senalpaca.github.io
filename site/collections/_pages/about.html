Hi, I'm Sena Dogan. I am an interdisciplinary developer interested in a variety of topics like digital media technologies, human rights, film and so on. 

For the projects I work on, my main focus is to combine important topics with digital architectures and draw a focus on environmental, political issues while making art. I aim to create experiences that trigger multiple sensory stimuli in people. For me this means to stay updated and continuously refresh my knowledge about different tools and techniques and combine these with my own skills.
