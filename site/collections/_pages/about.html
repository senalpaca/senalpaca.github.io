Hi, I'm Sena Dogan. I am an interdisciplinary developer based in Berlin. I am interested in a variety of topics like digital media technologies, human rights, film, photography and also their intersections.

For the projects I work on, my main focus is to combine important topics with digital architectures and draw a focus on environmental, political issues with my implementations. I aim to create experiences that trigger multiple sensory stimuli in people. For me this means to stay updated and continuously refresh my knowledge about different tools and techniques and combine these with my own skills.
